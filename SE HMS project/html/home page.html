All About Hospitals

A hospital is a place where a person goes to be healed when he or she is sick or injured. Hospitals also treat people who do not stay overnight, called outpatients. Doctors and nurses work at hospitals. Doctors make use of advanced medical technology to heal patients.

Doctors and Nurses may refer to a children's game of imaginative role-playing where medical profession roles are adopted and acted out. A nurse is a person who is trained to give care to people who are sick or injured. Nurses work with doctors and other health care workers to make patients better.
